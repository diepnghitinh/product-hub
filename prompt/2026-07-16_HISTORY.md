### Theming gotcha handled вҖ” `.report-workspace` token shadowing
The report workspace **redefines** shadcn tokens on its root to stay always-light: some as **valid HSL triplets** (`--input/--popover/--accent/--ring/--muted-foreground/--foreground` вң“) but `--primary`/`--border` as **full `hsl()`/hex** вҖ” which *breaks* `bg-primary`/`border-border` for anything rendered **inside** the workspace. So:
- The **trigger** (renders in-place, inside the workspace) uses only workspace-valid tokens вҖ” `border-input`, `ring-ring`, `text-foreground`/`muted-foreground`; **no `bg-primary`**.
- The **calendar** is **portaled** (escapes to app scope, where `bg-primary` etc. are valid) вҖ” exactly how the existing `OwnerSelect`/`SelectMenu` pickers already behave in the workspace, so no new inconsistency.

Verified: `tsc --noEmit` вң“ (caught + fixed a `boolean | null` on the day button) + `vite build` вң“.
